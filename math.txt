Advantages of Average (Mean)
Easy to calculate and understand.
Takes all data points into account, providing a comprehensive measure.
Useful for further statistical analysis.
Provides a central value that represents the dataset.
Widely used in various fields such as economics, business, and engineering.
Disadvantages of Average (Mean)
Affected by extreme values (outliers), which can skew the result.
Not suitable for skewed distributions.
Cannot be used for categorical data.
May not accurately represent the central tendency if the data is not normally distributed.
Requires numerical data, limiting its applicability.
